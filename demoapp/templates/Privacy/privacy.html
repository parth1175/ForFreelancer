Our Services
ApplyAway is a job search organizer and career services platform that helps job seekers organize their search across different platforms and also connects job seekers with Institutions (Universities and Career Centers) and provides each with powerful tracking and organizational tools. Our System is designed to provide job seekers and Institutions with control over their data. Job seekers can fully control their profile and can determine what information they see on their dashboard and share with other job seekers and Institutions.

ApplyAway does not share data with any third-party Organizations or Employers without your consent, and will never sell your personal data.

That is about you but individually does not identify you, such as your job preferences, education, experience, projects, details about your job search;
About your internet connection, the equipment you use to access our Services, including mobile data, and usage details.
Pertaining to which search engine our application and extension are accessed from.

We collect this information:

From third parties, for example, our business partners.

Disclosure of Your Personal Information
We only share your personal information with your consent. We will ask you before your profile is made public and before it can be shared with Insitutions or with another user. Should you consent to sharing your profile, Institutions will be able to view your profile and have access to your resume. With consent, Institutions will also have access to data coming from the Save Job! and Applied! buttons which will not be tied to any user’s identity.
You may be invited to use ApplyAway’s Services by an Institution. Should you choose to accept, your personal information will be shared with the Institution that invited you and staff from that Institution.
We may disclose information when relevant law enforcement bodies request it, if we believe that disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with the law, regulation, valid legal process or governmental or regulatory request. If we are going to release your data, we will do our best to provide you with notice in advance by email, unless we are prohibited by law from doing so.
Disclosure of Non-Personal Information
We may disclose non-personal information, aggregated information, and information that does not identify any individual, without restriction. This type of information cannot be reasonably linked back to our users and may be provided to our business partners as a way to track trends and view statistical data about job applicants.
